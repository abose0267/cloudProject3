BiteParty!

BiteParty is a React Native Application that I developed along with my friends, with the goal of solvng the problem of choosing a place to eat when going out with a group of friends. The app lets users set basic preferences such as radius, price range, and dietary restrictions. Once the user sets those filters, everyone in the group will see the same 10-15 restaurants and will swipe right or left depending on what they want

BiteParty!

BiteParty was built in React Native with a Firebase backend. Behind the scenes, during the course of each party, our app would take in data from each person in the party based on if they swiped right or left, and then calculate a score for each restaurant based on the swipes, and then would reccomend the restaurant with the highest score.

Zenith

Zenith is a mobile application that I developed with my friends that was designed to be a good mixture of Google Classroom and Remind. The app would act as a more efficient ways for students in classrooms or clubs to communicate with the club leaders or teachers. This communication would allow for the clubs/ classes to be run more efficiently, and with less mistakes on either side.
